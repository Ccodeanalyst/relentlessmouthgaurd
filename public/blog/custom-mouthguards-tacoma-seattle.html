Why custom fit matters for contact sports

Boil-and-bite guards are easy to find, but they are built for broad convenience. A custom-fit guard starts from an impression of your mouth, which helps create a more secure feel during training and competition. For athletes who are breathing hard, talking to coaches, moving under pressure, and taking contact, fit is not a small detail.

No mouthguard can prevent every injury, and no sports guard should be treated as medical or dental advice. Still, a properly fitted sports mouthguard may help reduce certain oral injury risks when it is worn correctly and replaced when damaged, loose, or worn down.
