SPORTS MOUTHGUARDS FOR CONTACT SPORTS

RELENTLESS Mouth Guards makes sports mouthguards for athletic use. We are not a dental practice and do not provide dental diagnosis, dental treatment, or medical advice.

Sports use only

Our products are intended as athletic sports mouthguards for contact and impact-risk sports, including boxing, MMA, Muay Thai, football, hockey, lacrosse, basketball, wrestling, grappling, and similar activities.

Not a dental provider

RELENTLESS Mouth Guards is not a dental office, dentist, orthodontist, oral surgeon, or medical provider. Product information on this site is not dental, orthodontic, or medical advice.

Not for treatment

Our sports mouthguards are not sold as night guards, bruxism guards, TMJ/TMD appliances, sleep apnea devices, retainers, orthodontic appliances, whitening trays, or treatment devices for any dental or medical condition.

No injury guarantee

No mouthguard can prevent all injuries, concussions, dental trauma, jaw injuries, cuts, or impact-related harm. Sports mouthguards may help reduce certain oral injury risks only when properly fitted, worn, and maintained.

Consult a professional

Speak with a licensed dentist, orthodontist, physician, or qualified healthcare provider before use if you have braces, dental work, implants, jaw pain, loose teeth, oral injuries, breathing issues, or any medical/dental concern.

Fit and care

Do not use a guard that causes pain, breathing restriction, choking risk, cuts, or an unsafe fit. Inspect your guard before each use, clean it after use, and replace it if worn, damaged, loose, or deformed.
