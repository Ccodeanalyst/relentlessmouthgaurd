# Month 1 SEO Roadmap

Last updated: May 20, 2026

## Positioning

RELENTLESS sells custom-fit, custom-graphics sports mouthguards for contact-sport athletes, gyms, teams, and programs. The first SEO month should prioritize local buyer intent around Tacoma and the greater Seattle area while also building a clean technical foundation for national/custom mouthguard searches later.

## Week 1: Technical SEO Foundation

Goal: make every public page clean, crawlable, and presentable in Google and social previews.

Tasks:

- Add unique meta descriptions to Home, Shop, Builder, Gallery, Contact, and Terms.
- Add canonical URLs for every public page.
- Add Open Graph and Twitter preview tags.
- Add `robots.txt`.
- Add `sitemap.xml`.
- Make sure `/admin/` and `/dashboard/` are not indexed.
- Add useful `alt` text for product/gallery images.
- Add base structured data:
  - `Organization`
  - `WebSite`
  - `BreadcrumbList` where page hierarchy exists
- Confirm the page heading structure is clean on each public page.
- Set up Google Search Console and submit the sitemap.
- Set up Bing Webmaster Tools and submit the sitemap.

Acceptance criteria:

- Each public page has a unique title and meta description.
- Sitemap resolves at `/sitemap.xml`.
- Robots file resolves at `/robots.txt`.
- Admin/dashboard pages are not intended for indexing.
- No important public page is blocked from crawling.

## Week 2: Local Landing Pages

Goal: create pages that directly match high-intent local searches.

Recommended pages:

- `/custom-mouthguards-tacoma/`
- `/custom-mouthguards-seattle/`
- `/team-mouthguards-tacoma-seattle/`

Primary location phrases:

- Tacoma
- Greater Seattle area
- South Sound
- Pierce County
- King County
- Washington

Content approach:

- Keep pages useful and specific.
- Speak to athletes, gyms, teams, coaches, and parents.
- Include product options, process, timeline, custom graphics, team/gym orders, and a direct CTA.
- Avoid duplicate city pages that only swap city names.

Acceptance criteria:

- Each local page has a unique angle and unique copy.
- Each page links to Shop, Design Your Guard, Gallery, and Contact.
- Each page includes Tacoma / Seattle service-area language naturally.

## Week 3: Buyer-Intent Content

Goal: answer practical purchase questions and support conversion.

Recommended pages or guides:

- `How Custom Mouthguards Work`
- `Single Layer vs Dual Layer Mouthguards`
- `How to Choose a Mouthguard for Contact Sports`
- `Custom Team Mouthguards for Gyms and Fight Teams`
- `How the At-Home Impression Kit Works`
- `Custom Graphics Mouthguards: Logos, Flags, Names, and Team Designs`

Sport-specific targets:

- MMA
- Boxing
- Muay Thai
- Football
- Wrestling
- BJJ
- Rugby
- Hockey

Acceptance criteria:

- Each page answers a real buyer question.
- Each page links toward a purchase or inquiry action.
- Pages avoid medical/dental treatment claims.

## Week 4: Measurement and Local Authority

Goal: measure search traction and start local trust-building.

Tasks:

- Review Search Console indexing and page coverage.
- Check for title/meta rewrite patterns.
- Add conversion events for:
  - Start Order
  - Add to Cart
  - Promo Apply
  - Checkout Start
  - Contact click or submit
- Build a local outreach list:
  - Tacoma fight gyms
  - Seattle boxing gyms
  - South Sound MMA gyms
  - Muay Thai gyms
  - youth football / contact-sport teams
  - coaches and sponsored athletes
- Prepare Google Business Profile if the business has a public/service-area setup ready.

Acceptance criteria:

- Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools are connected.
- Sitemap has been submitted.
- First set of local pages is indexed or requested for indexing.
- Outreach target list has at least 25 local prospects.

## What Not To Do In Month 1

- Do not publish thin city pages that only swap city names.
- Do not overpromise medical/dental benefits.
- Do not stuff keywords into hidden text, titles, or footers.
- Do not build a blog before technical and local foundations are in place.
- Do not index admin/dashboard or checkout-only utility pages.
