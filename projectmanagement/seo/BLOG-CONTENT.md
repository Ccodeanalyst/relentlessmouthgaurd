## Editorial Rules

- Do not overpromise medical or dental outcomes.
- Do not claim the company is a dental provider.
- Use real product photos, not generic stock imagery.
- Use exact keywords naturally in headings, opening paragraphs, image alt text, and internal links.
- Include one clear purchase or inquiry CTA per post.
- Add BlogPosting and BreadcrumbList structured data to each post.
